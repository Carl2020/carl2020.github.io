Max Temperature

Visualizations

The scatter plot above demonstrates the expected relationship between distance from the equator and maximum temperature. As this was compiled in the winter months, the temperatures are expectedly much lower as one goes increasingly north of the equator in winter. No doubt, in the Northern hemisphere's summer we would see the curved pattern starting low below the equator and arching sharply upward as one travels north.
